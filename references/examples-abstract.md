# Examples: Abstract Translation

Use these examples when translating abstracts. The abstract must follow the target journal format. If the journal format is unknown, use a provisional Background-Objective-Methods-Results-Conclusion logic and state that it is provisional.

## Example 1: Unstructured Abstract Logic

Chinese source:

```text
疾病B严重威胁人类健康，但其发病机制尚不清楚。本研究探讨A在疾病B中的作用。我们检测了A的表达并分析其与临床特征的关系。结果显示A在疾病B组织中升高，并与不良预后相关。A可能成为疾病B的潜在生物标志物。
```

Better SCI English:

```text
Disease B remains a major health burden, yet the molecular factors associated with its progression are incompletely understood. This study investigated the role of A in disease B by assessing its expression and its association with clinicopathological features. A expression was increased in disease B tissues and was associated with poorer prognosis. These findings suggest that A may serve as a potential biomarker for disease B, although further validation is required.
```

Journal-logic rationale:

- Use "associated with" for prognosis unless prediction is formally validated.
- Add "potential" and validation boundary.
- Avoid claiming mechanism when only expression and association are measured.

## Example 2: Structured Abstract Skeleton

Use this when the journal requires a structured abstract:

```text
Background:
[One sentence defining the field gap.]

Objective:
[One sentence stating what the study tested or evaluated.]

Methods:
[Design, sample/material, main assays, and analysis.]

Results:
[Primary result with direction, comparator, statistics if supplied, and key secondary result.]

Conclusion:
[Evidence-bounded conclusion and implication.]
```

Do not fill missing sample size, study design, statistical results, or validation cohort from inference.

## Example 3: Abstract Stop Point

Chinese source:

```text
本研究证实A可作为疾病B的临床诊断工具。
```

Must stop or downgrade unless supplied:

- Diagnostic cohort.
- Sensitivity, specificity, AUC, cutoff, validation set.
- Comparator or clinical reference standard.

Safe wording:

```text
These findings suggest that A warrants further evaluation as a potential diagnostic biomarker for disease B.
```
