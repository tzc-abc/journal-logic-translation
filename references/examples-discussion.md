# Examples: Discussion Translation

Use these examples when translating Chinese Discussion sections. Discussion should interpret findings, compare with prior work, show implications, and keep limitations visible.

## Example 1: Controlled Interpretation

Chinese source:

```text
本研究首次证明A是治疗疾病B的有效靶点，具有重要临床应用价值。
```

Problem:

- "First", "effective target", and "clinical application value" are high-risk claims.

Safer English:

```text
Our findings suggest that A may represent a potential target for disease B. However, further validation in independent cohorts and functional models is required before its clinical relevance can be established.
```

Journal-logic rationale:

- Downgrade "proves" to "suggest".
- Downgrade clinical utility to potential relevance.
- Add validation boundary without inventing data.

## Example 2: Relationship To Existing Knowledge

Chinese source:

```text
我们的结果与前人的研究一致，说明A在疾病B中发挥重要作用。
```

Better SCI English:

```text
These findings are consistent with previous reports implicating A in disease B and further support a role for A in this context.
```

Risk note:

- Cite the previous reports if the final manuscript includes references.
- Use "support a role" rather than "demonstrate an important role" when evidence is indirect.

## Example 3: Limitations Without Weakening The Paper Too Much

Chinese source:

```text
本研究仍存在一定局限性，未来需要进一步研究。
```

Better SCI English:

```text
This study has several limitations. First, [specific limitation]. Second, [specific limitation]. Future studies should validate these findings in [model/cohort/context] and clarify [mechanism/application] using [method].
```

Journal-logic rationale:

- Generic limitations look performative.
- Good limitations name what is missing and what experiment or dataset would address it.
